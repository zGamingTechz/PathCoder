About PathCoder

PathCoder is a personalized coding learning roadmap platform designed to help developers chart their unique coding journey. Whether you're just starting out or looking to sharpen your skills, PathCoder offers a tailored approach to help you succeed.

Key Features

  • Personalized Roadmap: Custom and Unique learning paths based on your experience level and coding preferences.
  • Task Management: Track and manage coding tasks in an intuitive to-do list system.
  • Discussion Room: Ask questions and chat with fellow users.
  • Leaderboard: To add a competitive edge and keep users motivated.
